Van and Truck Rentals - Easy moving solutions

Looking for Brisbane's easiest moving solutions? GTFO makes moving house and transporting your belongings as hassle free as possible.

With a range of vehicles and services, save yourself the stress of moving and let us help you get your things where they needs to go. Whether you're moving house or just clearing out the garden, we've got large and small vehicles at great rates.

Don't fuss around trying to reverse a trailer, our vans are easy to load, easy to drive and can fit heaps. At competitive short term rates, they're great for those odd jobs you can't get done with your car. Need to pick up some big IKEA flat packs, or maybe you found some bargain antique furniture on gumtree - we've got your back.

Thinking bigger? Our trucks are big enough to move just about anything, and with longer-term and one way hires available, they're ideal for moving house. All our vehicles are easy to drive and can be driven with just a regular drivers licence.

Our Services

Vehicle Dropoff / Pickup

We know your time is valuable, so have us deliver a vehicle anywhere in the Brisbane area and we'll pick it up when you're done. Just another convenience to make your rental as easy as possible.

Packing / Unpacking

Can't find the time or energy to move things yourself? Leave it to us! We can provide labour to help with loading and unloading. We'll get your items from point A to point B, whether it's your precious belongings or you're just getting rid of some old furniture.

One Way Hires

Save yourself the trip back. For a fee, we can get the vehicle from your destination back to our depot, so you don't have to. GTFO offer short-term, long-term and interstate hires.

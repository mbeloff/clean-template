Our Services

Looking for Brisbane's easiest moving solutions? GTFO provides services to make moving house or transporting your belongings as cheap and hassle free as possible.

Vans

GTFO vans offer an easy solution for transporting those items you can't fit in your car and are even modified with extra headroom to fit tall items. Short term hires from $30 per hour* make picking up that bargain furniture you found on gumtree easier than ever.

  • Drivers 21+
  • Car licence OK
  • Seats 2
  • ***(storage/weight capacity?)
  • Short term hires

Trucks

If you have a larger move in mind, trucks are also available - fully enclosed, and able to be driven on a regular licence, they make getting your belongings to their destination easy and safe. With short- or long-term hires, GTFO has you covered.

  • Drivers 25+
  • Car licence OK
  • Seats 3
  • ***(storage capacity?)
  • Short and long term hires
  • One way hires

Vehicle Dropoff / Pickup

We know your time is valuable, so have us deliver a vehicle anywhere in the Brisbane area and we'll pick it up when you're done. Just another convenience to make your rental as easy as possible.

Packing / Unpacking

Can't find the time or energy to move things yourself? Leave it to us! We can provide labour to help with loading and unloading. We'll get your items from point A to point B, whether it's your precious belongings or you're just getting rid of some old furniture.

One Way Hires

Save yourself the trip back. For a fee, we can get the vehicle from your destination back to our depot, so you don't have to. GTFO offer short-term, long-term and interstate hires.
